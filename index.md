## Lemma 1
For every graph $G$ with $|E(G)|\geq 1$, $thin(G) \leq pthin(G) \leq bandw(G)$. Moreover,
a linear layout realizing the bandwidth leads to a (strongly) consistent partition
into at most bandw($G$) classes. \[[1](#ref-1)\]
